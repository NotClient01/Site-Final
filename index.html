Revolutionizing Agriculture with Advanced Technology

Founded by two innovative University of Tennessee students, New Horizon Precision combines cutting-edge drone technology with artificial intelligence to deliver precision agricultural and land management services across East Tennessee and beyond.

We specialize in providing both recurring contract services and one-time solutions, offering our clients transparency through our dedicated customer portal and maintaining the highest standards of safety and efficiency.

Our mission is to help farmers, landowners, and agricultural businesses optimize their operations while reducing costs and environmental impact through advanced drone technology.
