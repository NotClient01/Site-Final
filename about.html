About New Horizon Precision

Pioneering the future of precision agriculture through innovative drone technology and sustainable practices

Our Story

From university innovation to agricultural revolution

The Beginning

New Horizon Precision was founded by two forward-thinking University of Tennessee students who recognized the immense potential of drone technology in revolutionizing agricultural practices. What started as a university project has grown into a comprehensive agricultural and land management service company.

Our Mission

We are committed to helping farmers, landowners, and agricultural businesses optimize their operations while reducing costs and environmental impact through advanced drone technology. Our precision approach ensures maximum efficiency and minimal waste.

Growth & Expansion

Starting in East Tennessee, we've expanded our services throughout the South, building lasting relationships with clients who value innovation, reliability, and sustainable practices. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted partner in agricultural advancement.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Innovation

We continuously embrace cutting-edge technology and methodologies to deliver the most advanced agricultural solutions available. Our commitment to innovation drives us to explore new possibilities in precision agriculture.

Sustainability

Environmental responsibility is at the heart of our operations. We help clients reduce chemical usage, optimize resource allocation, and implement practices that protect our land for future generations.

Precision

Every operation we perform is executed with meticulous attention to detail. Our advanced mapping and AI technology ensure that every application is precisely targeted for maximum effectiveness.

Reliability

Our clients depend on us for critical agricultural operations. We maintain the highest standards of safety, compliance, and operational excellence to ensure consistent, dependable service.

Transparency

We believe in open communication and clear reporting. Our customer portal provides real-time access to project data, ensuring clients always know exactly what's happening with their operations.

Partnership

We view our relationship with clients as a true partnership. We work closely with farmers and landowners to understand their unique needs and develop customized solutions that drive success.
